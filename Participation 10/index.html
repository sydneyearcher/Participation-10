My Process in Creating This Font

When creating this font I started off with basically nothing. No idea, no reference, just playing around with the many controls. It soon started to take shape of what I could only define as a retro font. It reminds me of the 50s in a way which was why I chose lime green for the background of this web page-a color I think goes hand in hand with the 50s. I also added a little picture to liven the site up which further prompted me to do a little remodel. The image reminded me of the Aphex Twin song QKThr, which influenced me to change up the lime green background to a more somber color. Hence the charmingly eerie website you have before you.
